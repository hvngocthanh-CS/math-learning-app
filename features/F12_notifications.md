# F12 - Notification & Reminder

**Phase**: 5
**Screen**: Global (hien thi tren moi man hinh)

## Mo ta

He thong thong bao va nhac nho hoc sinh hoc tap.

## Chuc nang chi tiet

### Reminder tu dong
- **Trigger**: Khi hoc sinh khong hoc trong 24h
- **Channel**:
  - Email thong bao
  - Web Notification (push notification tren trinh duyet)

### Notification Bell (Top Bar)
- Hien thi so thong bao chua doc
- Click mo danh sach thong bao
- Cac loai thong bao:
  - Nhac hoc bai
  - Thong bao dat thanh tich moi
  - Thong bao streak sap mat
  - Cap nhat bai hoc moi tu Admin
  - Thong bao tu Teacher/Parent

### Quan ly thong bao
- Danh dau da doc / chua doc
- Xoa thong bao
- Cai dat tuy chon nhan thong bao

## Navigation
- Notification Bell hien thi tren Top Bar moi man hinh
- Click thong bao -> di den man hinh lien quan
