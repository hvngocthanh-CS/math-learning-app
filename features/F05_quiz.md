# F05 - Practice / Quiz

**Phase**: 2
**Screen**: S-05

## Mo ta

He thong bai tap va kiem tra voi nhieu dang cau hoi, cham diem tu dong va phan hoi tuc thi.

## Chuc nang chi tiet

### Cac dang cau hoi
- **Trac nghiem**: Chon 1 dap an dung trong nhieu lua chon
- **Dien so**: Nhap dap an so vao o trong
- **Keo tha**: Keo dap an vao vi tri dung

### Cham diem & Feedback
- Cham diem tu dong sau moi cau
- Feedback realtime:
  - **Dung**: Animation chuc mung + Reward (sao/xu)
  - **Sai**: Giai thich chi tiet ngay lap tuc
- Hien thi ket qua tong hop sau khi hoan thanh

### Thu thach ngau nhien
- Tu dong tao de on tap tong hop
- Mix cac dang bai tu nhieu chuong
- Tang do kho dan theo trinh do hoc sinh

### Ket qua
- Diem so / So cau dung
- Sao thuong (1-3 sao tuy theo ket qua)
- Xu thuong
- Thoi gian lam bai
