# F03 - Student Dashboard

**Phase**: 1 (Uu tien cao nhat)
**Screen**: S-01

## Mo ta

Trang tong quan cho hoc sinh, hien thi thong tin hoc tap hang ngay va truy cap nhanh cac tinh nang.
Thiet ke moi grade se co bai hoc và bai tap khac nhau. Co 5 khoi là 1-5
## Chuc nang chi tiet

### Daily Mission Card
- Hien thi nhiem vu hoc tap trong ngay
- Trang thai: Chua lam / Dang lam / Hoan thanh

### Streak Counter
- Dem chuoi ngay hoc lien tuc
- Bonus reward khi dat streak milestone

### Mascot
- Nhan vat dong hanh
- Thay doi trang thai theo tien do hoc

### Recommended Lessons
- Goi y bai hoc tiep theo (AI suggestion)
- Dua tren tien do va ket qua hoc tap

### Quick Access
- Nut "Tiep tuc hoc" -> quay lai bai hoc dang do
- Truy cap nhanh cac tinh nang chinh

### Top Bar
- EXP Progress Bar (thanh kinh nghiem)
- Stars / Coins (so sao va xu hien tai)
- Mascot Avatar
- Notification Bell

### Side Menu (Icon-based)
- Home (S-01)
- Lessons (S-03)
- Games (S-06)
- Leaderboard (S-07)
- Shop (S-08)

## Navigation
- S-01 -> S-02 (Chon lop)
- S-01 -> S-03 (Danh sach bai hoc)
- S-01 -> S-06 (Game Lobby)
- S-01 -> S-07 (Leaderboard)
- S-01 -> S-08 (Profile & Shop)
